Mathematics Learning Center

Free math tutoring, online via Zoom or face to face in Heafey 223, is available through the Mathematics Learning Center. Set up a one-to-one appointment, sign up for a weekly study group, or come to a power up session to strengthen a specific skill. Students need to register ahead for these services. For quick last minute questions, try online drop-In tutoring 6 - 8 pm Sunday, Tuesday, Thursday evenings.
